My Favorite Places in Tangier

Restaurant Kebdani

Traditional restaurant Kebdani is known for its perfectly grilled meats, fragrant tagines, and comforting Moroccan classics served in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Address:

Rue Dar Baroud,
90000 Tangier, Morocco

What I like about it:

This is where I go when I want something truly Moroccan and full of flavor. The grilled specialties are outstanding, and the portions are generous.

Restaurant Antartique

Restaurant Antartique blends Moroccan flavors with Mediterranean finesse, offering beautifully plated fish, shellfish, and local specialties in a bright, welcoming setting.

Address:

Avenue Ben Abi Zaraa Braness,
90050 Tangier, Morocco

What I like about it:

The freshness is unbeatable. You can truly taste the ocean in every dish. I especially love coming here for a long, relaxed lunch.
